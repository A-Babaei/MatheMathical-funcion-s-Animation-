Current project

StatusDoctoral research · ongoingPrimary areaNeuromodulation and behavioral neuroscience

Parafascicular thalamic DBS in experimental Parkinsonism

An experimental and computational investigation of immediate stimulation-dependent behavioral responses in a unilateral 6-OHDA rat model.

Research question

How do stimulation parameters applied to the parafascicular thalamic nucleus influence motor and exploratory behavioral outcomes in a hemi-Parkinsonian model?

Experimental approach

Rodent lesion model, lesion validation, stereotaxic implantation, deep brain stimulation, structured behavioral testing, and video-based scoring.

Analytical contribution

Data organization, repeated-measures modeling, model comparison, uncertainty reporting, visualization, supplementary tables, and manuscript preparation.

Public status

Only high-level methods are presented here. Histology, quantitative findings, manuscript status, and publication links should be added after formal verification and disclosure approval.
