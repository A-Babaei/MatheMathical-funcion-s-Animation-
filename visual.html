04 / Visual work

Scientific clarity.
Visual discipline.

This page is structured for selected figures, graphical abstracts, educational media, video, websites, and branding. Only work cleared for publication or client display should be uploaded.

01Scientific figures

Graphical abstracts and experimental schematics

Use 3–6 strong, publication-ready examples with clear authorship and disclosure.

02Statistical visualization

Figures grounded in analysis

Display the outcome, uncertainty, sample unit, and model—not decoration alone.

03Educational media

Courses and technical presentations

Show curriculum structure, learning objective, sample slide, and instructor role.

04Video and motion

Educational and promotional production

Embed compressed previews or YouTube links; do not upload large source files to GitHub Pages.

05Web design

Technical and commercial websites

Explain the problem, information architecture, content strategy, SEO, and operational role.

06Identity systems

Logos and visual branding

Show the brief, design logic, deliverables, usage context, and permission to publish.

Display policy

Do not publish client assets, publisher-owned figures, identifiable laboratory personnel, or unpublished research material without authorization.

For AI-assisted work, state the tool and the extent of assistance. For adapted figures, identify the original source and licence.
